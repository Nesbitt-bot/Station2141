Step 1. Sign up for a JDoodle account.

![](image-1024x528.png)

Click **Register** to start.

![](image-1.png)

Step 2. After signing in, open the menu and choose **API**.

![](image-2-1024x456.png)

Free tier is fine — 200 files a day is plenty.

![](image-3-1024x528.png)

Pick **Free** and just take what you can get for free.

Step 3. Upload some default code and share it to your own page.

![](image-4-1024x615.png)

Click the three little dots, then save the code.

![](image-5-1024x550.png)

Click **Editable Share** to embed it on your page.

![](image-6-1024x546.png)

A URL will pop up — paste it into your page and you're done.

![](image-7-1024x595.png)

Create a new page in WordPress and select **Code Editor**.

![](image-8.png)

Paste it in like this. Replace `the url you just copied` with the actual URL you just got, then save.

```
<iframe src="the url you just copied" width="100%" height="720" frameborder="0" marginwidth="0" marginheight="0" allowfullscreen=""></iframe>
```

![](image-9-1024x670.png)

Open your page and you're up and running.
