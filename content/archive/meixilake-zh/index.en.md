![](IMG_6963-1024x768.jpg) The first time I came back here was to see an elementary-school classmate. We hadn't talked in a long time, and our lives had grown quite different. We didn't say much when we met — just rode our bikes together and slowly remembered things from before. *Stay in touch*, he said. The strange thing was that he seemed to know I was studying programming just by looking at me, while I had no idea how my own appearance had changed. ![](IMG_6958-1024x768.jpg) ![](IMG_6957-1024x768.jpg) Life is brief, and the people who pass through it slowly drift away. What they leave behind is a fragment of a fond memory — light, like water, but with a faint sweetness if you taste it carefully.
