![](IMG_4561-1024x768.jpg) The Xiang River is where I learned to ride a bike. Back when shared bikes were everywhere, my grandfather and I would play by the river on weekends. The long bike path made me want to find its end, but with limited time it was hard to reach by walking alone. So I learned to cycle, and finally got to explore the Xiang. ![](IMG_3666-1024x768.jpg) That fall, I learned to ride. I wandered along the long bike path beside the river — there are intertidal lagoons by the bank, and on the western shore the seasons each bring their own landscape. In winter I rode north, exploring the river's western bank. The path twisted and turned and felt like it would never end.
