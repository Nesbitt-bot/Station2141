## What is the class about

Organization will we like CSE 131

-   No lecture, but preparation is required!
-   Each module contains a studio (due by the end of the day assigned) and an assignment (due by the end of the day of the next module)

The material includes

-   Basic computer capabilities (I/O, esp. custom I/O)
-   Demystifying how computer systems operate
-   More than one machine, more than one type of the machine
